Daily Missions

This is a guide for you to get started.

Instructions:

1. Begin your day by jotting down memorable episodes that occurred to you. If there aren't any noteworthy personal experiences, choose a scene from a TV show or sitcom (preferably avoiding movie scenes due to their length).

2. Utilize the morning exercise to create a 2-page spread manga illustrating the situation you noted earlier. Dedicate 50 minutes to this daily practice.

3. Initiate the drawing process without relying on words to describe the situation. Focus on using actions, settings, onomatopoeia, and drawing. Perhaps get your inspirations from silent films.

4. As you become more proficient, consider the reading flow from left to right and top to bottom. Integrate text bubbles strategically and add words/lines to enhance the narrative.

This daily routine will not only improve your manga drawing skills but also sharpen your ability to convey stories visually.
