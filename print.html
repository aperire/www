Tips on Printing Manga

Make your physical copy!

When working on your manga file, it's crucial to pay attention to the margins and ensure that your content remains within the borders to avoid any risk of words or drawings being cut off. Working within the designated green box is the safest approach. Additionally, for optimal print quality, it is recommended to use a resolution of at least 300 dpi, with 600 dpi being preferable, although 1200 dpi is excellent for detailed work, albeit potentially heavy on your computer, especially if dealing with multiple pages. Since manga typically involves black and white illustrations, remember to set your canvas color mode to grayscale during the setup. Finally, when your project is ready, export the files in PDF format and send them to the printing shop for the best results.
